Why A Custom Theme?

Customized Solutions

Most themes come with generalized features that may not work for more unique websites. With a custom theme you can create solutions for your website built into the theme itself, and contrary to what most people think, custom coded themes are no different than other themes when it comes to making your own changes.

Cleaner/Faster Website

When building a normal WordPress website, developers tend to use themes made for mass distribution, and use lots of plugins that can slow down your website. When you create your own coded solutions you get a cleaner website that doesn't have unused, unnecessary features slowing it down.

Enhance Productivity

Most themes are too generalized to provide any real utility for any specific business. However, when creating your own theme every part of it will be tailored to you and your goals, and your website will be coded to help you be as efficient as possible.
